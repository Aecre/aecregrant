Aecre Grants Scheme

Aecre Grants are no-strings attached grants for any person with a project to improve our environment.

ABOUT THE GRANT

Apply for a grant

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. At least one £500 grant is awarded every three months, for a total of three grants, according to the following schedule.

  • 30th September 2018 - Applications now closed, soon to be announced.
  • 31st January 2018
  • 31st May 2019

Additional grants may become available thanks to other sponsors. If so, they'll be awarded according to the same schedule.

APPLY FOR THE GRANT

Sponsor a grant

If you'd like to sponsor a grant, here's how it works:

  • You must sponsor the full amount (£500)
  • We can either select a recipient on your behalf or share the applications with you. If you choose the latter, we will only share applications after receiving your sponsorship (to protect applicants' privacy)
  • To keep grants running smoothly, we will ask that funds are disbursed through us. Your grant will be awarded according to the schedule above, along with the other recipients

FAQ

Is this real?

Yes! At Aecre we'd rather share our profits with those who would put it to a good use, helping those with exciting projects they're passionate about that would benefit our environment. This grant scheme was inspired by Helium Grant

What type of proposals do you fund?

These grants are meant to serve as risk capital, so you can act upon whatever you can't stop thinking about. This could include: equipment or tools to do your work, time to work on a research project, tuition for a class or workshop, etc.

Creativity is encouraged! You can use the grant however you feel it furthers your goals. Just make sure you can explain to a stranger (us) why it's important.

We'll prioritise proposals where:

  • Funding goes towards an individual, not an organisation
  • Funding isn't readily available for this type of proposal
  • You've taken action already, or can otherwise demonstrate your commitment
  • You have an interesting backstory or unusual insight into your work
  • Impact extends beyond the grant's timeline
  • Grant timeline is shorter (ex. 3 Months) vs. longer (ex. 3 years)

When you say "no strings attached", what does that mean?

If you're selected for a grant, we will email you to arrange payment details. We'll send you a follow-up email to hear how it all went. That's it!

Can anybody apply?

Yes, any age, industry, job title, philosophy, or stage of life. The only requirement is that you must reside within the UK.

How will I receive the grant?

We will send the money through a bank transfer, or via an agreed method that suits you.

I'm outside of the UK, can I apply?

At the moment, we can only accept proposals for projects within the UK. We hope to open this up further afield in the future, sign up to our mailing list below for updates.

I have a question about applying for a grant. How can I get in touch?

Email us at grants@aecre.co.uk

How can I get updated on this project?

Sign up below. We'll email infrequently with updates on projects we've given grants too, and when new funding cycles are announced.
