Aecre Grants Scheme

Aecre Grants are no-strings attached grants for any person with a project to improve our environment.

ABOUT THE GRANT

Apply for a grant

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. At least one £500 grant is awarded every three months, for a total of three grants, acording to the following schedule.

  • 30th September 2018 - Applications now open
  • 31st December 2018
  • 31st March 2019

Additional grats may become available thanks to other sponsors. If so, they'll be awarded according to the same schedule.

Sponsor a grant

If you'd like to sponsor a grant, here's how it works:

  • You must sponser the full amount (£500)
  • We can either select a receipient on your behalf, or share the applications with you. If you choose the latter, we will only share applications after receiving your sponsorship (to protect applicants' privacy)
  • To keep grants running smoothly, We will ask that funds are disbursed through us. Your grant will be awarded according o the scheule above, along with the other recipients
